MI² Lab

Medical Imaging and Multimodal Intelligence Laboratory, NJU

Ophthalmic Imaging AI, Foundation Models, and Multimodal Large Models

We develop intelligent analysis methods, foundation models, and multimodal large models for ophthalmic medical imaging, including fundus, OCT, and OCTA data.

Eye-Heart-Brain Imaging, Multimodal Large Models, and Medical Agents

We study intelligent computing across eye, heart, and brain medical imaging, using multimodal large models and medical agents for clinical reasoning and workflow support.

Vascular Imaging Analysis and Intelligent Diagnosis

We analyze vascular imaging to model structure, function, and disease biomarkers, supporting intelligent diagnosis, treatment planning, and clinical translation.
