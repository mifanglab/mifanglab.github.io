# Graduate Studies

MI² Lab conducts research on cardio-cerebrovascular imaging analysis, ophthalmic medical imaging, medical foundation-model post-training, and medical agents. We focus on real clinical problems at the intersection of medicine and engineering, combining fundamental algorithm research with translational collaborations with clinical hospitals.

The lab is constantly looking for motivated graduate students who have solid mathematical skills and interests in computer vision and machine learning.

# Research Directions

Direction Focus
Cardio-cerebrovascular imaging analysis Modeling vascular structure, function, and disease-related imaging biomarkers.
Ophthalmic medical imaging Analysis and clinical applications based on fundus, OCT/OCTA, and related imaging modalities.
Medical foundation-model post-training and agents Medical-domain post-training, multimodal reasoning, tool use, and medical agents for clinical scenarios.

# Candidate Profile

  • Applicants interested in master's, regular PhD, or direct PhD programs at Nanjing University; 2027 undergraduate students with expected recommendation-based admission eligibility are encouraged to contact us early.
  • Clear interest in medical imaging, medical foundation models, or medicine-engineering interdisciplinary research, with strong coding ability.
  • Reliable, willing to commit to long-term research, and proactive in communication when facing problems.
  • Publication experience or open-source community contributions are a plus.
